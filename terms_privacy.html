1. What is DaySavvy?

DaySavvy is an Emotionally Intelligent AI Assistant™ that helps you manage tasks, goals, and well-being. By using DaySavvy, you agree to these terms.

2. What data do we collect?

  • Account info: username, full name, email, and password (securely hashed).

We use this data only to provide, secure, and improve DaySavvy. We do not sell your data or use ad networks.

3. How do we use your data?

  • To provide and personalize your DaySavvy experience.

We use trusted third-party services to power certain features (such as AI processing, email delivery, and authentication). These services receive only the minimum data needed to function and are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection agreements.

We do not share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data storage & security

  • We implement rate limiting and security controls to prevent abuse.
  • While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our protections.
